* **The Past (Father Christmas):** An ancient, weary, but warm voice. He remembers the old ways‚Äîcandles flickering in the quiet, the simplicity of handwritten letters, and the gentle hush of snowfall.

* **The Future (Mrs. Claus):** A calm, nurturing, ethereal voice. She whispers of a digital tomorrow where the care and love of the season persist, watching over us like a guiding star.
